Intelligence Search and Rescue Platform for Rescue Command Center

Allows the rescue commander to get a general view of the whole situation and make precise decisions.

PRODUCT OWNER

RESPONSIBILITY

UI/UX Design

TOOLS

OVERVIEW
The intelligece search and rescue system consist two parts: the platform for rescue commander and AR glasses application for firefighters. I was in charge of UI/UX design of the platform. A few times of adjustments were made after received feedback from user. In my opinion, the difficulty of this project is to show as many important information as possible and to make sure they are presented in a simple & neat way.
