SQUARE ONE

To enhance the overall equipment effectiveness(OEE) through collecting and analyzing the data of machine tool.

PRODUCT OWNER

TANGRAM, Foxconn

RESPONSIBILITY

UI/UX Design, Icon Design, Editorial Design (ex: user manual, DM)

TOOLS

Axure, Adobe Illustrator

OVERVIEW
Our client Tangram designs and manufactures IIoT sensors to collect data of machine tools. We collaborate with Tangram in order to build a platform that make uses of the data collected by sensors. One of the essential feature of SquareOne is to intergrate and show the data(including status, records) of machine tools, components and sensors at a glance which was difficult to fulfill in the past. Furthermore, it is possible to output monthly report in one click; which makes getting the accurate OEE easy and quick.
